Welcome to [App Name]. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. We do not have a central database (no backend), and most of your data stays on your device.

  • On-Device Data: Your focus tasks, XP, levels, and focus history are stored locally on your device (LocalStorage/UserDefaults). We do not have access to this information.
  • Usage Data: We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., when you start a session, unlock a level, or view a screen). This helps us improve the user experience. This data is linked to an anonymous Device ID.
  • Purchase Information: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. When you make a purchase, RevenueCat and Apple process your payment information. We do not store your credit card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To synchronize global focus cycles based on your device's time zone.
  • To track your progress (XP, Levels, and Streaks) locally.
  • To analyze app performance and usage patterns via PostHog.
  • To manage and verify your "Plus" or "Pro" subscription status via RevenueCat.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and payment processing.
  • PostHog: For product analytics. PostHog Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Since your focus data is stored locally, deleting the app from your device will permanently remove all your progress, tasks, and history. We cannot recover this data for you.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR in Europe or CCPA in California), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the data we collect. Since our data is anonymous and mostly local, the best way to exercise these rights is by managing your app settings or deleting the app.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
